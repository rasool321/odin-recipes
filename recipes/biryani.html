Authentic Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

For Marination:

  1. 500g chicken (bone-in, cut into pieces)
  2. 1 cup yogurt
  3. 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  4. 1 tsp turmeric powder
  5. 1 tbsp red chili powder
  6. 1 tsp garam masala
  7. 1 tsp coriander powder
  8. 1 tbsp lemon juice
  9. Salt to taste
  10. 2 tbsp oil

    For Rice:

  1. 2 cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  2. 4 cups water
  3. 2-3 green cardamoms
  4. 1 bay leaf
  5. 1-inch cinnamon stick
  6. 3 cloves
  7. Salt to taste

    For Cooking:

  1. 2 large onions (thinly sliced)
  2. 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  3. 2 green chilies (slit)
  4. 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  5. 1/2 cup mint leaves
  6. 1/2 tsp saffron strands (soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk)
  7. 3 tbsp ghee or oil
  8. 1/2 tsp garam masala

Step-by-Step Cooking Process

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

>In a large bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, lemon juice, salt, and oil. Add the chicken pieces and coat them well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (overnight is best).

Step 2: Prepare the Rice

Rinse the soaked rice and drain the water. In a large pot, boil 4 cups of water with cardamoms, bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, and salt. Add the rice and cook until 70-80% done (it should still be firm). Drain the rice and keep aside.

Step 3: Cook the Chicken

Heat 3 tbsp oil/ghee in a large pan. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown. (Save some for garnishing) Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies, and cook until soft. Add marinated chicken and cook on medium heat for 10-12 minutes until it releases oil. Mix in coriander and mint leaves.

Step 4: Layering the Biryani

In a heavy-bottomed pot, spread a layer of chicken at the bottom. Add a layer of half-cooked rice on top. Sprinkle some fried onions, coriander leaves, mint leaves, saffron milk, and a pinch of garam masala. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used. Pour 2 tbsp ghee on top.

Step 5: Dum Cooking (Slow Cooking)

Cover the pot with a tight lid (or seal with dough). Cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes. Turn off the heat and let it rest for 10 minutes before opening.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy!

Gently mix the layers and serve hot with raita (yogurt sauce) and salad. Enjoy the delicious aroma and flavors of homemade Chicken Biryani!
